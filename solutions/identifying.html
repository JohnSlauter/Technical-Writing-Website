Identifying Issues

In order to properly treat mental health issues and disorders, one must first be aware that they are present. Online mental health screening tests exist for depression, anxiety, psychosis, and several other issues that have been exhibited by the population more frequently since the start of the pandemic. These tests ask whether you’ve experienced various symptoms linked with a certain issue and to what extent. While by no means are these tests a valid substitute for a professional diagnosis, they are quick and easy ways to determine whether or not one should take the time to seek professional help. Periodically assessing your own mental health can also help to prevent the onset or relapse of a mental illness. To take an online mental health assessment on Mental Health America, which has been endorsed by several physicians, one can: 1) go to their website, 2) select the quiz that they believe applies to them 3) complete the test, and 4) Take the “Mental Health Next Steps” quiz once finished with the first quiz.
