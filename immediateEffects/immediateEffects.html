Immediate Effects

This section of our report covers the immediate effects the pandemic on society, such as the loss of in-person jobs, closures, and restrictions.

Loss of In-Person Jobs     Transition from in-person jobs to online jobs, loss of service jobs, and other effects.

Closure of Schools and Colleges     Effect of school closures on students and family, changes in education.

Restrictions on Social Gatherings     Preventative measures to reduce the spread of COVID-19 and the subsequent mental effects.

Uncertainty Regarding Prognosis of Pandemic     Uncertainty and anxiety surrounding the constantly changing guidelines and knowledge about COVID-19.
